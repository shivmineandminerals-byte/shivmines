# Off-Page SEO Playbook — Shiv Mines and Minerals

> Backlinks and citations are **earned off-site** — they cannot be coded or fabricated.
> Buying links, link farms, or spam directories = **Google penalty / deindexing**. Do NOT use them.
> This playbook is the white-hat path: real business listings, real citations, real outreach.
> Work top-to-bottom. Most of Week 1 can be done by one person in a few hours.

---

## 2. Google Business Profile (HIGHEST PRIORITY — do this first)
This drives Maps + "near me" + local pack, and is a trusted citation.
1. Go to business.google.com → create/claim "Shiv Mines and Minerals" at the Karauli address.
2. Verify (postcard/phone/video as offered).
3. Fill 100%: categories above, hours (Mon–Sat 9–6), phone, website, the medium description.
4. Add 10+ real photos: the mine, plant, stockpiles, product samples, the lab certificate.
5. Add Products (each grade) and Services.
6. Post weekly updates (new grade, an FAQ, a photo).
7. **Ask every happy client for a Google review** — this is the single biggest local lever.

## 3. B2B marketplaces + directories (real leads AND citations)
Create a full profile on each (free tiers are fine). Use the NAP + descriptions above.
Priority order:
1. **IndiaMART** (indiamart.com) — the dominant B2B channel in India; list every product.
2. **TradeIndia** (tradeindia.com)
3. **ExportersIndia** (exportersindia.com) — important for export queries.
4. **Justdial** (justdial.com) — local + citation.
5. **Sulekha** (sulekha.com)
6. **IndiaBizClub / Fibre2Fashion / Go4WorldBusiness** (mineral/industrial B2B)
7. **Connect2India / Alibaba** (if pursuing export)
8. **Google Maps** (via GBP), **Bing Places**, **Apple Business Connect**

For each: complete profile, add all products with the descriptions, link to the matching page on your site
(e.g. link their "glass sand" listing to https://shivmines.in/products/glass-sand).

## 4. Industry & association citations
- Join/list with mineral & mining associations (e.g. FIMI – Federation of Indian Mineral Industries, state mining associations).
- Foundry & glass industry bodies (e.g. Institute of Indian Foundrymen) — supplier directories.
- Rajasthan state industry/MSME directories.
These are high-trust, topically-relevant links competitors often miss.

## 5. Linkable assets (already on your site — promote them to earn links)
You already have things worth linking to. Point people to them:
- **/quality** — real independent lab analysis (buyers and journalists cite data).
- **/guides/silica-sand-grades-explained**, **/guides/silica-sand-specifications** — reference content.

## 6. Digital PR / outreach (the compounding lever)
- **HARO / Featured / SourceBottle:** answer journalist queries about mining, construction materials, glass, solar — earn editorial links.
- **Guest articles:** offer a "how to choose silica sand" or "silica for solar glass" piece to construction/foundry/industrial publications.
- **Supplier features:** ask existing B2B customers to list you as a supplier on their site.
- **Local press:** a Karauli/Rajasthan business or a capacity/certification milestone is a local-news angle.
Outreach email template:

## Weekly cadence (first 90 days)
- **Week 1:** Deploy · GSC + Bing sitemap · Google Business Profile (verified, 100% complete).
- **Week 2:** IndiaMART + TradeIndia + ExportersIndia + Justdial full profiles.
- **Week 3:** 5 more directories · request first 10 Google reviews.
- **Week 4+:** 1 association/industry listing per week · 1 outreach/guest pitch per week · GBP post weekly.

## What NOT to do (these cause penalties)
- ❌ Buying backlinks or "1000 backlinks for ₹500" gigs.
- ❌ Auto-submitting to hundreds of junk directories.
- ❌ Private blog networks (PBNs), link exchanges at scale, comment/forum spam.
- ❌ Fake reviews or fake review schema.
White-hat is slower but it's the only thing that ranks and stays ranked.
